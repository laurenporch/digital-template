Little Grower

  • High Concept: Collect the light and water orbs to make the plant grow!
  • Player Experience Goals: Learn the fun of growing things.
  • Genre: Platformer.
  • Game play: Control Linette using the arrow keys to go left, right, and jump (Up arrow) in order to collect all of the light and water orbs.
  • Prototype goal: Seeing something grow based on player actions to test whether it is a motivating factor.
  • Features: You get to see the the flower grow through your efforts!
  • Setting/Premise: Linette is a farmer, but her plants aren't ordinary. Instead of growing on regular sunlight and rain water, they only grow on magic! Help Linette collect the magical light and water orbs and watch her flower grow!
  • Story: See Setting/Premise.
  • Target Audience: Children.
  • Play time: ~30 seconds.
  • Strategies: Collecting all the orbs on the ground first.
